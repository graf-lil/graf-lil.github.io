Welcome

Welcome to my personal website! I am currently working as a PostDoc at the TU Dortmund, Center for Research on Education and School Development (IFS) in the interdisciplinary project FAIR. I obtained my PhD in psychology at the VDS CoBeNe and was working as university assistant at the Department of Developmental and Educational Psychology at the University of Vienna. I am conducting empirical research in the field of education, mainly using quantitative methods. My main research interests are academic emotions, civic education, and political socialization.

On this website, you can find my CV, a list of publications and contributions to conferences, and an overview of courses and workshops I taught up to now. If you are interested in my research, have any questions or would like to collaborate, feel free to reach out via e-mail.
